FAQ

Who is this for?

Carrying too much on your mind? Can't really tell your friends everything because of trust, judgment, or just timing. This is for you.

Is this like therapy?

Nope. Therapy is serious work — fixing things, working through problems. Here, it's just a safe place to unload, vent, and be heard. No judgment, no pressure, no homework.

Why not just use an app or AI?

Apps and AI are cool, but they can't truly listen. A real human under a short NDA gives a level of privacy and attention no algorithm can match.

What if my German isn't good enough?

No problem. We speak English and German, and you can mix them if you like. The point is to be understood, not perfect.

Do you record or take notes?

Never. No recordings, no notes, no transcripts. What you say stays between us.

Who will actually listen to me?

Always a real human being. No apps, no bots, no AI — just 100% analog ears, fully present.

What if I'm judged for what I say?

That won't happen. We don't criticize, we don't judge. You can be messy, angry, sad, funny — it's all welcome.

How does it work?

Pick a time. Show up. Speak your mind. We both sign a short NDA, so you know your words won't go anywhere else.

How private is this really?

Safer than apps or AI. We don't collect personal data, don't link your email or payment to what you say, and don't store anything.

How much does it cost?

For students: €10 per hour. Cash only for now — simple, transparent, no hidden fees.
